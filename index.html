Research

I work with NEMO-based high-resolution models of the Mediterranean Sea, investigating interannual to decadal variability, anthropogenic influences such as river damming, and the dynamics of circulation and mixing.

Key topics

  • High-resolution Mediterranean ocean modelling
  • Lateral mixing, eddies, and numerical noise
  • Impact of Nile and other rivers on circulation and salinity
  • Potential tipping behaviour in regional climate systems
